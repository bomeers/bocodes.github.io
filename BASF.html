BASF Rebrand

For this project, I, along side a group, was tasked with creating an un-official rebrand of the company, "BASF"

The Card

Having simplicity in mind, I created this card to have a sleek look and feel. The tagline, "create chemistry" was created by the group involed with rebranding this company.

The First Draft

This is the first draft of the logo that I came up with. I wanted to have the basf be legible while keeping a flow-like feeling to it.

The Second Draft

For this draft, I added in shading to the "A" and "F" in order to make the four letters more noticeable.

The Final Draft

I reverted back to the first draft created and gave the logo a square, phone app, look and feel.

The Final Design

The final logo was created with the help of my co-partner Nick Carter.
